# PayFi Box Pitch Copy

## One-Line Positioning

**ZH**  
企业继续用法币付款，平台自动完成稳定币转换与全球结算。

**EN**  
Businesses continue paying in fiat while the platform automatically handles stablecoin conversion and global settlement.

## Short Product Intro

### 50-word version

**ZH**  
PayFi Box 是一个面向企业的智能支付编排平台，帮助企业继续使用法币付款，同时由平台在后台完成稳定币转换、全球结算、流程追踪和审计管理。

**EN**  
PayFi Box is an enterprise payment orchestration platform that lets businesses keep paying in fiat while the platform handles stablecoin conversion, global settlement, process visibility, and audit management in the background.

### 100-word version

**ZH**  
PayFi Box 是一个面向企业跨境支付与全球结算场景的智能支付编排平台。企业前端继续按照熟悉的法币流程付款，平台则在后台完成稳定币转换、支付执行、流程编排和审计追踪。它解决的不是单一支付动作，而是企业从付款、结算到留痕审计之间的整条资金链路，并支持通过 API 和 MCP 接入企业系统与 AI agent。

**EN**  
PayFi Box is an intelligent payment orchestration platform built for enterprise cross-border payment and global settlement workflows. Businesses keep using familiar fiat-based payment flows on the front end, while the platform handles stablecoin conversion, payment execution, process orchestration, and audit visibility in the background. It is designed to solve not just a single payment action, but the full operational money flow from payment initiation to settlement and post-payment review, with API and MCP access for enterprise systems and AI agents.

## What AI Enables

**ZH**  
AI 在 PayFi Box 中不是简单的聊天入口，而是用于理解支付意图、生成流程建议、解释支付状态、总结审计信息，并通过 API 和 MCP 连接企业系统与 AI agent。AI 负责帮助企业更高效地发起、理解和协调支付流程，而资金状态、账务状态和执行状态仍由确定性的系统规则控制。

**EN**  
AI in PayFi Box is not just a chat layer. It is used to understand payment intent, generate workflow suggestions, explain settlement state, summarize audit activity, and connect enterprise systems and AI agents through API and MCP. AI helps teams initiate, understand, and coordinate payment workflows more efficiently, while fund state, ledger state, and execution state remain governed by deterministic system rules.

## Why It Matters

**ZH**  
传统跨境支付常常存在成本高、到账慢、路径不透明、对账和审计复杂等问题。PayFi Box 通过“法币支付 + 稳定币结算 + 流程编排 + 审计追踪”的组合方式，帮助企业在不改变支付习惯的前提下，获得更高效、更透明、更可编排的全球结算能力。

**EN**  
Traditional cross-border payment flows often suffer from high cost, slow settlement, weak visibility, and complex reconciliation and audit work. By combining fiat payments, stablecoin settlement, workflow orchestration, and audit traceability, PayFi Box helps enterprises gain a more efficient, transparent, and programmable global settlement layer without forcing them to change how they already operate.
